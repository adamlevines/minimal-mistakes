About UsPermalink

We are a group of researchers based at Oakland University who are committed to: working on socially relevant research problems using state-of-art NLP methodology; learning from one another to become better researchers, engineers, and educators; effectively communicating our findings to the research community and general public; engaging in ethical research practice; and providing a supportive work environment for all team members.
